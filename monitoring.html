What is the class size?

Our class sizes are designed to foster an interactive learning environment. Typically, classes range from 15 to 25 students, ensuring personalized attention and active participation.

How is progress assessed during the courses

Progress is assessed through a combination of assignments, exams, and practical projects. Our instructors provide timely feedback, and we encourage a holistic approach to evaluation, focusing on both academic understanding and practical application of the material.

Can I audit a class or attend a trial session before enrolling?

Yes, we offer opportunities for prospective students to audit a class or attend a trial session. It allows you to experience our teaching style, interact with instructors, and get a feel for the course content before making a commitment. Contact our admissions department to schedule a trial session.
